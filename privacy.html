Privacy Policy

Alpaca Space Colony
Bundle ID: com.mv2009.alpacacolony

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Alpaca Space Colony ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our idle clicker game on iOS and on the web. We do not operate user accounts or collect data on our own servers.

1. Information stored on your device

The App saves game progress and preferences locally on your device using browser or app storage (for example, localStorage). This data stays on your device unless you delete it.

This may include:

  • Game save data (wool, upgrades, planets visited, achievements, play time, prestige progress)
  • Settings (sound effects, music, haptic feedback, HUD layout preferences)
  • Whether the Ad-Free in-app purchase is active on this device

We do not receive this local data. You can remove it at any time using Wipe Save in the App settings, or by uninstalling the App and clearing site data in your browser.

2. Information processed by third parties

On the iOS version of the App, certain features rely on third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob (advertising)

The iOS App may show interstitial and rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising data, and usage information to deliver and measure ads.

On iOS, the App may ask for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before using your data for personalized advertising. You can decline tracking; the App will still work, though ads may be less relevant.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

RevenueCat and Apple (in-app purchases)

The Ad-Free purchase is processed through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat helps verify and restore purchases. Apple and RevenueCat may process purchase history, transaction identifiers, and an anonymous customer ID linked to your Apple ID for purchase validation and restore.

We do not receive your payment card details. Apple handles all payment processing.

Learn more: RevenueCat Privacy Policy · Apple Privacy Policy

Google Fonts (web version)

When you play the App in a web browser, fonts may be loaded from Google Fonts. Google may receive your IP address and basic technical data when those resources are requested. The native iOS App bundles fonts locally and does not load them from Google at runtime.

3. How we use information

  • Local data: to save your progress and remember your settings
  • Ads: to show optional advertisements that support development (unless Ad-Free is active)
  • Purchases: to unlock Ad-Free mode and restore previous purchases

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use a separate analytics SDK in the App.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

If you use the iOS App, you may see a system prompt asking whether the App can track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising. This is optional. Your choice is managed by iOS and can be changed later in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact us and we will help you address it with the relevant provider.

In-app purchases and advertising should be used with appropriate parental supervision.

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Local game data: kept until you wipe your save, clear app/browser storage, or uninstall the App
  • Third-party data: retained according to Google, Apple, and RevenueCat policies

7. Your rights (EEA, UK, and similar regions)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, or to object to certain processing. Because we do not operate our own accounts or servers, most requests about ad or purchase data should be directed to the relevant third party (Google, Apple, or RevenueCat). For questions about this policy or local data in the App, contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
